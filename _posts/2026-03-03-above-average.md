title: "Above Average"
date: 2026-03-03
---

Over the years having interacted with many people, there are very few things that I think I am above average at. I am not the best at them or top of class. But I am above average among the people who do them.

1. Running
2. Climbing
3. Chess
4. Cooking (more than half the world cooks, so this might seem like a bold claim, but I am a pretty good cook and if you random sample 100 people who cook, I will be better than 50 of them)
5. Coding/Logic
6. Memory (I can remember things/numbers from long ago and recall them with surprising accuracy)
7. Data Interpretation and analysis and finding the story in the data and simplifying them.
8. Exploring new things, learning new things and taking risks. I have huge risk appetite. 

That is pretty much it. In everything else I am average or below average. The last 4 are the ones that has helped me in my career. And the first 4 are the ones that has helped me settle anywhere in life.
