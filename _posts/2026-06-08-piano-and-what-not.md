Back in 2008 during my software developer training days in Mysore, I borrowed my brother's guitar. I already knew how to code, so I spent most of my time in the campus exploring new things. I started rock climbing, playing tennis and figured I would try to learn to play the guitar. 

But apart from me, everyone else in my batch used my guitar. I never figured how to play it properly. Spencer taught me like super basic stuff and that is pretty much it. 

Recently, I figured I would try to learn a musical instrument properly. Of course Guitar was the obvious choice. But a long conversation with Gemini kind of convinced me that Piano is the way to go. 

So I bought a cheap digital keyboard. Is it the standard 88 keys. The left and right hand independence is pretty tough. But after a few days of practice, I am getting better.

I found this Youtube Channel called [Pianote](https://www.youtube.com/@PianoteOfficial). It is pretty good. I loved [their one video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tEtukfFv3Wk) which takes you from zero to 20% within like 20 minutes.
